About me

A 2023 graduate from the City University of Hong Kong, majoring in Information Systems and achieving Summa Cum Laude honors - the top 1% of my class. My academic journey has been a global adventure, marked by valuable experiences at institutions like Erasmus University Rotterdam (Netherlands), Fudan University (Mainland China), and Strathclyde University (the United Kingdom). My early years unfolded in New Zealand, where I attended Glendowie College for high school. Passionate about exploring different cultures, I find joy in continuously expanding my horizons.

I started working at 18, getting into e-commerce, digital marketing, and CRM systems. These experiences have shaped my understanding of the intersection between technology and business, fostering a passion for innovative solutions and strategic thinking. In 2023, I got the Innovation and Technology Scholarship Award (the top 15 students from the finest eight universities in Hong Kong). This serves as my commitment to contributing and making a difference to drive positive change in the InnoTech industry and society through technology.
